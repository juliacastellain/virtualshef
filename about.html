About us


VShelf was created by Julia Castellain, a reader that, just like you, felt the need of a platform where you could log the books you're reading, already read and wants to read. Thanks to a task the creator was given in her WDB (WEB Development Fundamentals) class, she than put the idea into practice, creating a social network to keep track of readings, as well as rating books, making reviews and sharing likes and dislikes with friends and people around the world.


Are there books to buy or download at VShelf?

No! The Virtual Shelf is nothing but a social network, a place where you can track your readings and your ideas about them. We do not sell or provide books.

Do I have to pay to have a VShelf account?

Nope! We are an entirely free social network.

Ok, so, what can I do at VShelf?
  • Log the books you've read, are reading, intend to read and those you've abandoned;
  • Log the books you have or want to have;
  • Mark your favorite books and make lists;
  • Rate the books from 1 to 10;
  • Make reviews;
  • Follow people and groups with common interests;
  • Follow your favorite authors and publishers.
